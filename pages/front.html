Happy Place is a new project by HappyChap Creative Co. We're a B-Ham creative agency founded in 2010, and we just moved into a BIG space right in the heart of downtown. We're looking for complementary businesses, teams, and individuals to share it with.

Why move, start, or sequester part of your business at the Happy Place?

We think it's a damn fine space:

  • What a location!

    Smack dab in downtown Bellingham's shopping & business district. We're based in an up & coming part of Cornwall and we aim to liven things up around here.

  • A Walloping 2,400 Square Feet space

    The room is tall and long: it sure is nice to have room to breathe! Happy Place is ripe for retail and office space scenarios. 

  • Grade-A Suite-Mates, those HappyChaps

    The space is shared with us – The HappyChaps, an easy-going creative agency. *Proximity discount / free water-cooler consulting for office-mates.

  • Fetching Storefront & Front Architecture

    Plenty of windows provide ample walk-by display space. Lots of retail display potential for the front of your store along with a unique stage / lounge area.

  • Breakneck-ish Internet Speeds

    Forget Comcast. We get fast, reliable internet from a local ISP, Pogozone internet services. Whether you need wi-fi or wired ethernet, we'll hook you up to the Matrix.

  • Turnkey Retail Display & Fixtures

    Slat walls galore, grid-walls to boot, a looooong display case, vertical walls as far as the eye can see, shelves and more.

  • A “Heavenly” Mural & Creative Vibe

    Many thanks to our space's former tenants Innate & artist Yale Wolf for the legacy painted on the wall.

  • Flexible Space Use & Lease / Partnerships

    We don't rent through an agency or property manager, and we're willing to work with folks who are a good fit to find a way to cohabit.

  • Dog-Friendly Environment

    Bring your friendly pals in when visiting or sharing the space with us. Our resident mutt, Sammy, needs some company.

Who's a good fit for the Happy Place?

A million Possible Uses

  • Studio / Agency HQ

    We love creatives and would happily share our place with another team who vibes well with us.

  • Retail / Apparel / Craft Shop

    Sell your widgets/gizmos in our ready-to-go retail section. A funky, friendly storefront would be a great fit for our place.

  • Art Gallery

    High ceilings + big space = LOTS OF ART. Bring your gallery downtown and help us liven up this joint!

  • Events & Presentations

    Our space comes with a raised platform/stage that could be used for all manner of gatherings.

  • Coworking Space

    The HappyChaps found our stride in a coworking space and we would happily share our big new home with one!

  • Homebrew Supplier

    Hops and grains? Cauldrons and carboys? Sure, you can fill up our space with those. We'll even work in trade.

  • Curator of Fine Slippers

    The chaps agree: when your toes are cold, it's nice to have slippers. We think cold toes are an under-served market.

  • Specialty Music/Book Store

    Peddle your vinyl records, hand-made instruments, and out-of-print books in our new digs.

  • Virtual Reality Arcade

    VR gaming is the future: our abode would make an excellent spaceport, zombie-infested train, or laser battlefield. 

  • Vintage Toy Boutique

    Kids these days play too many darn video games: what ever happened to Lincoln Logs?

  • Your crazy/brilliant business or idea?

    We're pretty open-minded. If you are interested in the space, drop us a line!
