Space Amenities

  • Downtown Location!

    Located on Cornwall Avenue across from the Leopold Hotel we're smack dab in downtown Bellingham's shopping & business district.

  • Plenty of Room

    Over 1,300 sq. ft. of space with high (18 foot) ceilings allow for plenty of room to gather.

  • Raised Platform / Stage

    The space features a 13′ x 10′ raised wood stage equipped with a projector screen and plenty of power outlets.

  • Retail / Gallery Ready

    Mesh, metal grates and slat-walls adorn the space and make for easy set-up and displaying.

  • A “Heavenly” Mural & Creative Vibe

    Our space has been creatively beautified by Yale Wolf, featuring a large mural of Bellingham Bay with a big neon sign reading "HEAVEN" (a reference to the Herald sign).

  • In-House Photo Booth

    Happy Place tennant, The Photo Treehouse can provide a fun photobooth for your event.

  • Furnishings Provided

    We currently have a variety of folding chairs, fixed chairs, tables, etc. for use at no extra cost.

  • Dog-Friendly Environment

    Dogs are welcome at your hosted events - so long as they can behave.
